# Self-Host

> Источник: https://docs.twenty.com/developers/self-host/self-host

> Deploy and manage Twenty on your own infrastructure.

## Overview

Twenty can be self-hosted on your own infrastructure, giving you full control over your data and deployment.

## Why Self-Host?

- **Data ownership**: Keep all CRM data on your own servers
- **Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements for data residency
- **Customization**: Full access to modify and extend the platform

## Getting Started

Two primary deployment options are available:

1. **Docker Compose** — Quick setup with Docker
   - See: [docker-compose.md](../self-host/docker-compose.md)

2. **Cloud Providers** — Deploy on AWS, GCP, or Azure
   - See: [cloud-providers.md](../self-host/cloud-providers.md)
